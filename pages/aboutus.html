Our History...

Creeps Archery began in 1992 when Christian Atkinson created the company based on his first compound bow with Solocam technology. But the seeds to one of the industry’s most successful archery companies began many years earlier thanks to McPherson’s passion.
Atkinson brothers have been building bows for more than 50 years. They built their first stick bow when Christian was 10 years old and his first compound bow three years later. He couldn’t afford to buy one. Innovation and determination guided them, along with a willing shop teacher in school who allowed them to create a bow. Although Atkinson C. didn’t know it at the time, his life’s trajectory was set.
After founding Creeps Archery, Atkinson C. was determined to maintain control over every aspect of the building process. Mathews sources the best materials and micromanages the manufacturing process to deliver innovation, high-quality and craftsmanship recognized by hunters and competitive shooters.
